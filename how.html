Your Complete AgroCare Experience

Follow these simple steps to protect your crops using AI, weather insights, and government support.

📝Account Setup

Create Your Account

Sign up with your name, phone number, and location. AgroCare uses your profile to personalise crop recommendations, regional weather data, and relevant government schemes.

Free Registration Secure Login Profile Saved

📷Disease Scanner

Upload Crop Image – Get Disease Percentage

Take a clear photo of your crop leaf or stem and upload it to our first AI model. The system analyses the image and instantly returns a disease severity percentage — telling you how infected the crop is.

Upload any photo Instant % result Multiple crops Real-time AI

🔬Disease Identifier

Upload Again – Get Disease Name & Prevention

Run your photo through our second AI model for a deeper diagnosis. This model identifies the exact disease name, explains its symptoms, and provides step-by-step prevention and treatment advice tailored to your crop.

Exact disease name Symptom details Treatment steps Pesticide guidance

🌤️Weather Forecast

View 16-Day Weather Forecast

AgroCare integrates live weather data for your exact location. See the next 16-day forecast — including rain probability, temperature highs and lows, humidity, and wind — so you can plan irrigation, spraying, and harvesting at the right time.

16-day forecast Rainfall alerts Humidity & wind Location-based

Access Government Yojana Schemes

Browse government agricultural schemes (Yojana) integrated directly into AgroCare. Find subsidies, crop insurance programs, loan benefits, and support initiatives relevant to your region and crop type — all in one place.
